To join our chapter, simply fill out the contact form on our Connect Us page or reach out to our membership coordinator at membership@ieeecss.org. There's a nominal membership fee that provides access to all our events, workshops, and resources. New members are welcome throughout the year!

We organize a variety of events including technical workshops, guest lectures from industry experts, coding competitions, hackathons, networking events, and social gatherings. We also host the annual "Future of AI" conference and provide mentorship programs for students interested in various tech domains.

No, you don't need to be an IEEE member to join our Computer Society Student Chapter. However, IEEE members receive additional benefits and discounts on our events. We welcome all students interested in computer science and technology, regardless of their IEEE membership status.

We're always looking for passionate volunteers! You can volunteer for event organization, content creation, social media management, or technical support. Contact our volunteer coordinator at volunteer@ieeecss.org or attend our monthly volunteer meetings to learn about current opportunities.

Members enjoy access to exclusive workshops, networking opportunities with industry professionals, mentorship programs, internship referrals, certification courses at discounted rates, and the chance to develop leadership skills by participating in our committees. You'll also become part of a global community of computing professionals.
